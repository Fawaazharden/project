FAQ

The questions you'd ask on the call.

Why is this so cheap? What's the catch?

We don't have ReSimpli's overhead. They fund sales, marketing, exec comp, and investor returns. We're a small team. We charge for the setup work, then you pay your own raw API costs (30 to 50 times cheaper than their markup). No recurring product, no catch. The ongoing relationship is your lifetime Slack line.

What if ReSimpli changes their API and breaks it?

We patch it for free, for as long as you run the system. You have a private Slack line straight to us. No time limit, no retainer, no "out of support window" surprise. ReSimpli's API has been stable for 2+ years, but if it shifts, we handle it.

Will ReSimpli ban my account for this?

No. ReSimpli supports API access on Pro and Enterprise plans. We use the same endpoints their own AI agents use internally. No scraping, no auth bypass, no terms violation.

Can the agents really do my qualifying and disqualifying?

Yes. During setup we build your exact qualifying questions and disqualification rules into the agent. It transfers hot leads live, books appointments, runs drip nurture on the cold ones, and filters junk so your team stops wasting time. All to your spec.

I have a team. Will this work multi-user?

Yes. Conversations tag to specific ReSimpli users so reporting stays clean, and routing is configurable. AI handles first response, then assigns hot leads to the right rep with a summary in ReSimpli.

Will my reps see anything different in ReSimpli?

No. AI replies appear in ReSimpli's existing SMS threads, scoring updates the lead status field, summaries land in notes. One interface, same as today. No new logins, no training.
